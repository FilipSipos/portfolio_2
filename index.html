Who is Filip?

Flip is a fast learning, jazz loving, tea drinking European with roots in the Czech Republic, a heart in Australia and a home in the United States. He loves fly fishing, is not affraid of Loch Ness or ice cold water.

Filip takes ice cold showers, teaches Kundalini yoga and in the short moments between days and nights he likes to meditate. Flip questions the status quo and uses liminal thinking to form his believes. He loves innovative ideas and change.

If he is not answearing his phone and you can't get hold of him it means only one thing. He is drinking cup of tea with Shiuwen Tai at Floating Leaves Tea. Drinking good pu-erh tea and listening to a great jazz are two of Filip's favorite social activies.

Flip is laser focused but his mind is as broad as night sky. When somthing burdens his spirit instead of turnnig a TV on (which he doesn't have) he grabs his tent and climbs a mountin.

Last but not least he is happily married to his talented wife Anja. Filip and Anja met in Australia, she fell in love with his cooking and he got enchanted by her kind heart. Those of you who were lucky enough to meet Anja in person knows that she is the best testimony to Filip's name.
