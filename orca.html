ORCA PLUS

The ORCA card is all you need to pay your fare on buses and trains in the Puget Sound region.

After you load E-purse (electronic purse) value or a monthly pass on it, your ORCA card works like cash or a pass, automatically tracking the value of different fares and transfers so you don’t have to.

The Brief

Orca Card wants to reduce the amount of cars on the roads and become more environmentally friendly by using public transit for their daily commute. Currently, the Orca Card site is the only online access point away from the kiosks and retail locations, which is not mobile friendly. The site also does not have the structure to support easy access to information or card management.

Aspects to Consider

  • What are the current pain points of Orca Card users when using the website?

  • What unmet needs do users have which might be met by a mobile app?

  • What information is most important for commuters?

The Team

My Role

I was responsible for leading the team through the research, creating a wireframes and a clickable prototypes for usability testing. I was also appointed to be a project lead responsible for conflict resolution.

The Challenge
